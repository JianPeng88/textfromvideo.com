Frequently Asked Questions

What is TextFromVideo?

TextFromVideo is a powerful AI transcription platform offering unlimited audio and video text conversion services. This tool can accurately transform audio content in more than 98 languages into written text, with an impressive level of precision.

How much does it cost?

TextFromVideo is currently completely free of charge, so please feel free to use it without hesitation!

Is TextFromVideo truly unlimited?

Yes! TextFromVideo is indeed unlimited. There is absolutely no limit on total usage, with our customers typically processing hundreds of hours of content monthly. We have just one basic requirement: please do not share your account credentials or access with others.

Which audio/video formats do you support?

TextFromVideo is compatible with virtually all mainstream audio and video file types, covering common formats like MP3, M4A, MP4, and MOV, while supporting audio standards such as AAC, WAV, OGG, and OPUS, as well as traditional video formats including MPEG, WMA, WMV, and AVI, and even professional media formats like FLAC, AIFF, ALAC, 3GP, MKV, WEBM, VOB, RMVB, MTS, TS, QuickTime, and DivX.

Can I export my transcripts?

Absolutely! Your transcripts can be downloaded in various formats including: PDF documents, DOCX files, subtitle formats (SRT/VTT), CSV data sheets, and plain text TXT files. Additionally, our batch processing feature enables you to export multiple files simultaneously, significantly enhancing your workflow efficiency.
